About Me

As long as I can remember I've always been fascinated with how things were created. This curiosity led me to become a computer technician and I spent many years improving my craft. I was able to disassemble complicated machines and help others understand how they worked. I took on many roles within companies but always found satisfaction in finding out why something worked or why it didn't work.

Naturally my curiosity led me to troubleshooting software and diving into how lines of code interacted with hardware to deliver a user experience. Changing and tinkering with code was not enough for me, I wanted to create. I focused my attention into gaining more knowledge and spent thousands of hours learning and practicing. I have since embarked on a journey to never stop learning and creating. I am always looking forward to the next project and I would love to discuss any topic related to technology or programming, feel free to reach out.
